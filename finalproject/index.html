About Dying Light

Dying Light is a zombie survival game where you the player go around as Kyle Crane helping the people of Harran. In Dying Light you will go on many missions, but in order to survive you must find weapons scattered through out Harran, but in order to get to some of these weapons you must climb, duck, and run to get to what you need such as weapons, resources, and story items.
